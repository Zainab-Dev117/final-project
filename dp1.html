Chia Berry Energy Bowl

A delicious and nutritious energy bowl made with chia seeds, fresh berries, and Greek yogurt.

ingredients with proportions:

  • Organic chia seeds (3 tablespoons)
  • Fresh strawberries (½ cup)
  • Blueberries (¼ cup)
  • Unsweetened Greek yogurt (1 cup)
  • Raw honey (1 tablespoon)
  • Almond slices (1 tablespoon)
  • Pumpkin seeds (1 tablespoon)

Benefits:

  • High in fiber and protein
  • Quick and easy to prepare.
  • Provides omega-3 for heart health
  • Packed with vitamins and minerals.
  • Seeds and nuts support heart health.
  • Rich in antioxidants for immune support
  • Gives natural energy without sugar crashes.
  • Berries provide antioxidants for overall health.

Quality Tips:

  • Choose unsweetened Greek yogurt
  • Select raw honey for natural sweetness
  • Use fresh, organic berries for best taste
  • Soak chia seeds longer for an even creamier texture.
  • Add a sprinkle of cinnamon or cocoa powder for extra flavor.
  • Swap Greek yogurt with plant-based yogurt for a vegan version.

Detailed recipe:

The Chia Berry Energy Bowl is a wholesome, protein-packed snack or breakfast that combines creamy Greek yogurt with nutrient-rich chia seeds and fresh berries. To prepare, start by mixing 3 tablespoons of organic chia seeds into 1 cup of unsweetened Greek yogurt. Stir thoroughly so that the chia seeds are evenly distributed. Cover the mixture and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight for best results, allowing the chia seeds to absorb the liquid and thicken, creating a creamy, pudding-like texture.

Once the chia mixture is fully set, it’s time to add the toppings. Gently layer ½ cup of freshly sliced strawberries and ¼ cup of plump blueberries on top for a burst of natural sweetness and vibrant color. Drizzle 1 tablespoon of raw honey over the berries to enhance the flavor while keeping it healthy. For added texture and nutrition, sprinkle 1 tablespoon of almond slices and 1 tablespoon of pumpkin seeds on top, giving each bite a satisfying crunch. Serve the Chia Berry Energy Bowl chilled for a refreshing and energizing treat. Rich in protein, fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants, this bowl is perfect for a nourishing start to the day or a mid-day pick-me-up.
