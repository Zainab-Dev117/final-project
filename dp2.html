hailey bieber's viral pizza toast

A crispy, cheesy and flavor-packed toast inspired by Hailey Bieber’s simple yet trendy snack. Perfect for quick cravings and cozy evenings.

ingredients with proportions:

  • Thick sandwich bread slices (2)
  • Pizza sauce (or homemade tomato sauce) (4 (2 tbsp per slice))
  • Mozzarella cheese (freshly grated) (1 cup (½ cup per slice))
  • Pepperoni slices (8–10)
  • Dried oregano (½ teaspoon)
  • Chili flakes (¼ teaspoon or adjust to taste)
  • Fresh basil leaves (4–6)
  • Olive oil (1 teaspoon optional drizzle)

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Super quick and easy
  • Crispy edges with gooey cheese center
  • Perfect snack or light meal
  • Trendy & Instagram-worthy

Quality Tips:

  • Use freshly grated mozzarella instead of pre-shredded cheese for better melt and texture.
  • Lightly toast the bread before adding toppings to keep it crispy.
  • Choose a thick, sturdy bread slice so it holds the sauce and cheese well.
  • Use good quality pizza sauce with rich tomato flavor (not too watery).
  • Add fresh basil after baking for better aroma and freshness.

detailed recipe

Start by preheating your oven to 180°C (350°F). Place two thick sandwich bread slices on a baking tray and spread about 2 tablespoons of pizza sauce evenly over each slice. Sprinkle freshly grated mozzarella cheese generously on top, then arrange 4–5 pepperoni slices per toast. Lightly season with dried oregano and chili flakes for extra flavor.

Bake for 8–10 minutes or until the cheese melts completely and turns bubbly and slightly golden. Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, and drizzle a little olive oil if desired. Serve hot and enjoy a crispy, cheesy, and flavorful pizza-style snack.
