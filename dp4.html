Chocolate Kiwi Pops

A fun and refreshing treat made with juicy kiwi slices coated in smooth melted chocolate. Perfect for summer cravings and healthy dessert lovers.

ingredients with proportions:

  • Fresh kiwi (2 peeled & sliced thick)
  • Dark or milk chocolate (1 cup melted)
  • Wooden sticks (6–8)
  • White chocolate (2 tablespoons for drizzle, optional)
  • Coconut flakes or crushed nuts (2 tablespoons optional topping)

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Sweet and tangy flavor combination
  • Easy no-bake dessert
  • party perfect
  • Looks cute & aesthetic

Quality Tips:

  • Use firm but ripe kiwi for better slicing.
  • Choose good quality chocolate for smooth coating.
  • Let chocolate cool slightly before dipping for thicker layer.
  • Place pops on parchment paper while chilling.
  • Refrigerate until chocolate is fully set before serving.

detailed recipe

To make Chocolate Kiwi, peel 2 fresh kiwis and slice them into thick, even rounds, then gently insert wooden sticks into each slice. Melt 1 cup of dark or milk chocolate until smooth and glossy using a microwave or double boiler. Dip each kiwi slice into the melted chocolate, ensuring it is fully coated, and place it on parchment paper. If desired, drizzle 2 tablespoons of melted white chocolate over the top and sprinkle 2 tablespoons of coconut flakes or crushed nuts for added texture. Refrigerate for 15–20 minutes until the chocolate sets completely, then serve chilled for a perfectly sweet and tangy treat.
