Protein Iced Coffee

A smooth and refreshing iced coffee blended with protein for the perfect energy boost. Ideal for busy mornings, post-workout fuel, or mid-day productivity.

ingredients with proportions:

  • Fresh brewed coffee (1 cup chilled)
  • Vanilla or chocolate protein powder (1 scoop)
  • Cold milk or almond milk (1 cup)
  • Ice cubes (1 cup)
  • Honey or zero-calorie sweetener (1 teaspoon optional)
  • Cocoa powder (1 teaspoon optional)

Why You’ll Love It:

  • Perfect pre or post-workout option
  • Provides energy and taste without excess sugar or calories.
  • Served over ice, it’s a perfect cool drink for hot days or after workouts.
  • Helps keep you full longer and supports muscle repair, making it ideal for fitness enthusiasts.
  • Requires minimal ingredients and preparation time, ideal for busy schedules.

Quality Tips:

  • Use freshly brewed and fully chilled coffee for best taste.
  • Plant-based milks like almond or oat milk work well for a creamy texture.
  • Choose a high-quality protein powder that blends smoothly.
  • Shake well in a shaker bottle to avoid lumps.
  • Add ice last to keep texture thick and cold.
  • Adjust sweetness according to your fitness goals.
  • Customize with your favorite flavorings like chocolate, vanilla, or cinnamon.

detailed recipe

Protein Iced Coffee is a perfect snack-time beverage for anyone looking to boost energy and protein intake simultaneously. To prepare this refreshing drink, start by brewing a strong cup of coffee and allow it to chill for a few minutes. In a shaker or blender, combine one scoop of your preferred protein powder, such as vanilla or chocolate, with a small amount of cold milk to create a smooth mixture without lumps. Next, pour the chilled coffee into the protein mixture and gently stir or blend until fully combined. Adjust sweetness to taste by adding honey, maple syrup, or a sugar substitute, and optionally sprinkle cocoa powder or cinnamon on top for added flavor. Serve the drink over ice cubes in a glass for a cold, revitalizing experience. This protein-packed iced coffee is not only delicious but also ideal as a pre-workout boost, a quick breakfast option, or a healthy afternoon snack that keeps you energized.
